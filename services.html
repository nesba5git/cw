Specialized Treatment

Specialized Services

🧠

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing is an effective, evidence-based approach to help process and heal from traumatic experiences and PTSD. It works by guiding the brain to reprocess memories, making them less emotionally overwhelming.

ADHD Evaluation
