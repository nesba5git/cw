Our Story

Why we
started this

After working one on one in the mental health field for several years, it was easy to see where our health care system was failing. We witnessed such distinct barriers to successful clinical treatment that we felt compelled to create the change.

It was a warm May evening in 2017—after months of discussion and planning we were ready to do what we could to facilitate change. We did not want to see people who were unable to obtain mental health services due to insurance and financial issues. For us, this was the night Clear Water Wellness, LLC was born.
