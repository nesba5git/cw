Our Approach

Therapy should
feel accessible

At Clear Water Wellness we believe that therapy should feel accessible and approachable to everyone. And we know that the beginning steps of therapy are not always as clear as they could be.

So we try our best to make the process as transparent, welcoming, and smooth-sailing as we can! Continue scrolling for a helpful breakdown of what to expect as you embark on your therapy journey.

Step 1

Request an
appointment

Fill out our easy Initial Appointment Request form. You will be prompted to enter some basic contact information, a brief description of why you are seeking counseling, and your preferred availability for appointments.

You will also have the chance to enter any other info you feel would be important or helpful for us to know. Submit the form and you will be contacted within the week to discuss a time/date for your first appointment, the therapist you'll be assigned to, and payment.

Request appointment now →
Step 2

Complete your
client forms

Once your first appointment is set up you will receive an email containing necessary forms that must be filled out prior to your initial visit with us.

Keep your eye out for an email from Clear Water Wellness Client Portal with the subject line "Welcome from Clear Water Wellness" that will invite you to sign-in to your Client Portal to fill out your forms. These documents are very easy to access and complete.

Your Client Portal also allows you to request appointments online and use our Telehealth (virtual counseling) feature.

Step 3

Show up for
your first visit

We are located at 4 Park Plaza, Wyomissing PA 19610. We have plenty of free parking! You will know you are in the right place if you have found the building with the large number '4' out front.

We are located on the first floor, so come inside the building's main front doors and wait in the lobby. Your therapist will come out to retrieve you at your appointment time. We do appreciate if you send us a text to let us know that you have arrived.

Step 4

Continue your
therapy journey

At the end of your first appointment, your therapist can schedule you additional appointments. Some individuals schedule once a week, others every two weeks—it's up to you!

Some elect to schedule ahead only one appointment while others elect to have recurring appointments that enable them to secure time slots weeks or months in advance. We recommend the latter, especially if your preferred time-slot is a popular one! You can always cancel online if needed.

Please note you will always receive a text message reminder 24-hours before each appointment.

→

Virtual Option

If you can't attend in person, you can always attend virtually via video link.

→

Direct Contact

You can text or call your therapist through their HIPAA-compliant line anytime.
