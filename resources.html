Help When You Need It

Crisis & Resources

Mental health resources, crisis hotlines, and community support for Berks County and beyond.

24/7 Support

Crisis Hotlines

These resources are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Primary Resource

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Call or Text: 988

Free, confidential support for people in distress, prevention and crisis resources. Available 24/7 in English and Spanish.

Text Support

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Free, 24/7 crisis support via text message. Connect with a trained crisis counselor.

LGBTQ+ Support

Trevor Project

1-866-488-7386

Crisis intervention and suicide prevention for LGBTQ+ young people under 25. Also available via text (text START to 678-678).

Veterans

Veterans Crisis Line

988, then press 1

Confidential support for veterans, service members, and their families. Chat online at VeteransCrisisLine.net.

Domestic Violence

National Domestic Violence Hotline

1-800-799-7233

Confidential support, resources, and safety planning for those affected by domestic violence.

Substance Use

SAMHSA National Helpline

1-800-662-4357

Free, confidential treatment referrals and information for substance use and mental health disorders.

Crisis Navigation
