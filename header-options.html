Option A

Grouped Dropdowns (5 items)

Group related pages into dropdown menus. Reduces visible items from 8 to 5 while keeping all content accessible.

Dropdown contents:
• Get Started → What to Expect, Rates & Insurance, FAQ

Pros

  • Clean, uncluttered appearance
  • Logical groupings help users find content
  • Matches your current Squarespace structure
  • Scales well on mobile

Cons

  • Requires extra click to see all options
  • Some users prefer flat navigation
Option B

Minimal Navigation (4 items)

Ultra-clean approach with only essential top-level links. Secondary pages accessible through those landing pages or footer.

Services About Resources Contact Get Started

Page structure:
• Services → Links to Team, Support Groups, Rates within page
• About → Team page with links to others
• Resources → Blog, FAQ, Crisis Resources
• Contact → Includes "What to Expect" info

Pros

  • Maximum visual clarity
  • Very easy to scan
  • Modern, confident aesthetic

Cons

  • Some pages harder to find directly
  • Requires good internal linking
  • May need to restructure some pages
