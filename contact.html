Common Questions

FAQ

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Contact us with your insurance information and we'll verify your coverage.

How long are sessions?

Individual sessions are typically 50 minutes. Couples and family sessions may be 60-90 minutes depending on needs.

What if I need to cancel?

We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations. Life happens—just let us know as soon as you can.

Is everything confidential?

Absolutely. Everything shared in therapy is confidential, with very limited legal exceptions we'll explain upfront.

How do I know which therapist to choose?

We'll help match you! During your initial call, we'll learn about your needs and recommend the best fit.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes, we offer secure video sessions for those who prefer the convenience of therapy from home.

Prefer to talk?

Sometimes it's easier to just pick up the phone. We're happy to chat.
