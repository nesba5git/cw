Our Philosophy

Making therapy
less awkward

We know getting set-up with therapy isn't always the most straightforward, so we strive to make the process as easy as we can. We believe that therapy should feel accessible and approachable to everyone.

We are dedicated to providing support in a world where it feels difficult to find. We have created a safe and comfortable environment for our clients to feel secure enough to do the hard work on themselves.

How It Works

Getting started
is simple

We try our best to make the process as transparent, welcoming, and smooth-sailing as we can. Here's what to expect as you embark on your therapy journey.

01

Request an Appointment

Fill out our easy Initial Appointment Request form with your contact info and availability.

02

Complete Client Forms

You'll receive an email to access your Client Portal and fill out necessary forms before your visit.

03

First Session

Get to know your therapist and discuss what brought you to counseling and your goals.

04

Continue Your Journey

Schedule weekly or bi-weekly sessions. We'd love to have you as part of our Clear Water family!

Survivors of Suicide Support Group

If you have lost a loved one by suicide, you are not alone. This group provides a safe place for survivors to share their experience, learn healthy coping skills, and find community with others who understand.
